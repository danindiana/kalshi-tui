Added immediately before the MAE gate. Catches LSTM runaway predictions before any
downstream calculation can amplify them into phantom edges.

```python

pred_dev_pct = abs(prediction - current_price) / current_price * 100

    print(f"[SKIP] Prediction deviation too large: ...")
    # logs run with 0 opportunities and returns
```

## Known Limitations / Next Steps

1. **Backtest uses synthetic market prices** — `backtest_gates.py` estimates market prices
   from historical volatility, not actual Kalshi bid-ask. Real edges may differ.
   Fix: integrate live Kalshi market price feed into backtest replay.

2. **Delta model MAE ≈ naive baseline** — the model's $25.40 MAE is essentially the same
   as just predicting 0 change ($25.42 naive). It's not yet adding predictive value beyond
   the volatility estimate itself. Watch for this in live results.

3. **Outcome tracking** — no mechanism to mark contracts as WIN/LOSS after settlement.
   DB only tracks `placed` status. P&L is unknown until tracked manually.
   Fix: poll Kalshi `/portfolio/trades` after each settlement to record outcomes.

4. **`daily_loss()` tracks deployment cost, not P&L** — the $8.46 "loss" is the cost
   of contracts, not realized P&L. Actual loss may be less (if some contracts settled in-the-money).
